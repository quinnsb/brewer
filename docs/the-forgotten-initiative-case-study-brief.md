# The Forgotten Initiative Case Study Brief

Date initiated: July 16, 2026

## Status

First build implemented at `projects/the-forgotten-initiative.html` and linked from `work.html`.

## Working Brief

- Audience and desired hiring decision: Creative directors, in-house marketing leaders, agencies, and mission-driven organizations should see Quinn as someone who can lead strategy while staying close to the creative work.
- Secondary audience: Nonprofit and ministry leaders who need sophisticated communications without losing the human heart of their mission.
- Client/project: The Forgotten Initiative.
- Timeframe: June 2021 through March 2024.
- User role: Creative Content Producer from June 2021 to July 2023; Communications Director from July 2023 to March 2024.
- Collaborators and credit: Collaborative work with Jami Kaeb, executive leadership, donor development, communications staff, direct reports, freelance contractors, podcast guests, TFI Advocates, churches, foster care agencies, and people from the foster care community.
- Scope: Communications strategy, creative direction, brand messaging, video, podcasts, social, email, web content, blog writing, graphic design, audience development, Advocate recruitment, donor communications, fundraising campaigns, event promotion, merchandise, analytics/reporting, and team/contractor management.
- Problem and stakes: The foster care community can feel overlooked, while churches may care but not know how to begin or how to relate to local foster care agencies. TFI needed communication that made an emotionally sensitive mission understandable, personal, and actionable.
- Core constraint: Emotional sensitivity, multiple audiences, vulnerable stories, collaborative approval, many channels, and ongoing production alongside launches and fundraising.
- Central insight: Awareness needed to lead somewhere.
- Guiding principle: Make people feel seen, help them understand, and give them a meaningful way to respond.

## Outcomes and Proof

- Verified: The Forgotten Podcast surpassed one million total downloads during Quinn's tenure.
- Verified context: In January 2024, the website recorded approximately 4,750 organic search clicks and 229,000 search impressions.
- Verified context: In December 2023, the website recorded approximately 4,670 organic search clicks and 206,000 impressions.
- Qualitative proof: Quinn was promoted from Creative Content Producer to Communications Director.
- Qualitative proof: Major initiatives were publicly launched, including Foster Care in the Church and Just Neighbors.
- Qualitative proof: Podcast brands and content remain publicly available.
- Caution: Search metrics are useful context but should not be attributed solely to Quinn's work.
- Caution: Donation totals, email growth, Advocate recruitment numbers, social growth, YouTube growth, and conversion rates need verification before publication.
