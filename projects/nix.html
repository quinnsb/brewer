Serious consulting doesn't have to look like a stock photo.

Nix & Company untangles gnarly technical problems for companies that have usually tried everything else first. Their expertise is elite; their old brand was a blue gradient and a handshake photo. We gave them an identity with the confidence — and the wit — their work deserves.

The hook: nix, as in to cancel, cross out, delete. The brand celebrates removal. Strikethroughs, redlines, and a bold editorial mark that literally crosses out the problem. It's a one-liner that never stops paying off across decks, docs, and swag.
