A new name and a brand built for the long feature.

They came to me as a two-person production company named after a street they no longer lived on. They were ready for bigger projects, bigger crews, and a name that could headline a festival laurel. We started from zero: strategy, naming, identity, site.

Foxglove — a flower that's beautiful, a little dangerous, and impossible to ignore — set the tone for everything that followed. A cinematic wordmark, a title-card type system, and a palette graded like their footage: deep shadows, warm highlights.
