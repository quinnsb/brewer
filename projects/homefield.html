Changing how production is done, for the better.

Homefield is a video production studio with crews around the world and a simple belief: every shoot goes better when everyone's playing for the same team. The brand needed to bottle that feeling — the roar of a home crowd — and apply it to an industry that usually runs on call sheets and cold coffee.

We leaned all the way into the sports metaphor: pennants, badges, varsity type, and a roster system that celebrates the crew in every city. The result is a brand that makes clients feel like season-ticket holders and makes freelancers fight to get drafted.
