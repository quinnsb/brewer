Privacy Policy · Effective July 15, 2026

Privacy, without the fog.

Brewer is a portfolio and independent creative practice operated by Quinn Brewer. This policy explains what happens when you visit the site or choose to get in touch.

The short version

Brewer does not use advertising trackers, analytics pixels, user accounts, or a marketing database on this site. Brewer does not sell personal information or share it for targeted advertising.

Information you choose to send

If you contact Brewer, you may provide your name, email address, company or project information, and anything else you include in your message. The contact form on this site does not submit information to a Brewer database or server. It prepares a message in your own email application, and nothing is sent unless you choose to send it.

Messages that you send may be used to respond to you, discuss or provide services, keep ordinary business records, and meet legal obligations. Correspondence is kept only as long as reasonably needed for those purposes.

Routine technical data

Like most websites, the hosting provider and services used to deliver fonts or code may automatically receive limited technical information needed to serve the site, such as an IP address, browser or device type, requested page, and time of access. This site currently loads resources from Google Fonts and a third-party content delivery network. Their handling of technical data is governed by their own policies.

Brewer does not use that technical information to identify visitors, build profiles, or target advertising.

Children under 13

This is a general-audience professional portfolio. It is not directed to children under 13, and Brewer does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13.

If you are under 13, do not use the contact form, email Brewer, or send any personal information through this site. If Brewer learns that personal information from a child under 13 has been received, it will be deleted promptly. A parent or guardian may request deletion using the contact information below.

Your choices

You may ask what personal information Brewer has about you, request a correction or deletion, or withdraw from future communications. Certain records may be retained where reasonably necessary to complete a transaction, resolve a dispute, or comply with law.

Outside links and changes

This site links to websites and social platforms that Brewer does not control. Their privacy practices may differ. This policy may be updated when the site or its practices change; the effective date above will be revised when that happens.

Questions, privacy requests, or a concern involving a child under 13? Email quinnsb@gmail.com.
